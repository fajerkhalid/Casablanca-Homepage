Visit Casablanca, Morocco

Top three activities to do at Casa

Visit Historical/Religious
places

The Hassan II Mosque is the
largest mosque in Africa and is
the 14th largest in the world.

Take a stroll around
the urban markets

Tour around as you find
traditional handcrafted kitchenware,
snacks/drinks, clothings, and more.

Try out authentic
Moroccan dishes

Some popular Moroccan dishes are
Tagine, Cous Cous, Harrira,
and more which are often served
with Moroccan Mint Tea "Atay"
